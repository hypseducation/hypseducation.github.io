    Our Mission

    The mission of the HYPS EDUCATION is to actively promote international and intercultural understanding and to enrich the academic and
    cultural environment for Chinese students and for international institution.  HYPS EDUCATION brings qualified Chinese students to the world and the world to China.

    Core Values – the context within which we do our work

    We value being a major contributor to our mission of fostering critical thought and responsible global citizenship.
    We value students and their willingness to step beyond the safety of what they already know.
    We value what international students and scholars bring to the learning environment. 
    We value education in all forms; growth experiences; students learning from students; personal development; transformation.
    We value internationalism, intercultural awareness and communication, diversity and respect for other people and cultures.
    We value tolerance, open-mindedness and forward-thinking.
    We value creating world peace one student at a time.

    Our Services

    With extended knowledge of China market, HYPS EDUCATION differentiate itself with unique services that
    help our partners achieve sustainable growth in the ever challenging competition.

    Services for Our Partner Institutions:
    • Take necessary steps to promote partner institutions and their programs in China.
    • Take reasonable methods in confirming the accuracy of information provided by prospective students in the application.
    • Make efforts to provide program counseling for prospective students and recruit well-qualified students for enrollment in language training,undergraduate and graduate programs.
    • Arrange visits to prestigious high schools and universities in China for the purpose of establishing partnerships with them.
    • Develop donation or fund-raising strategies and organize Meet & Greet events in China.
    Services for Students:
    Through its worldwide network of offices, HYPS EDUCATION goes to extra lengths to ensure that students
    are fully supported during their studies overseas. These support and supplementary services include:
    • Initial and basic counseling services provided for those making enquiries via websites, e-mail or the
      phone, or during visits to HYPS EDUCATION offices, seminars or exhibitions.
    • Assistance by experienced and highly qualified HYPS EDUCATION Planners to help students and
      parents identify courses and institutions that would best suit their needs.
    • Proactive assistance in selecting appropriate schools and education programs for further studies.
    • Applications to selected schools are completed on behalf of students.
    • Passport documentation and visa applications are prepared and lodged on behalf of students.
    • Travel, accommodation and airport transfer arrangements are arranged for students.
    • Pre-departure orientation is provided to give realistic expectations, which minimizes 'culture shock'
      and ensures successful settlement.
    • Arrival services include: airport reception, general orientation, accommodation placement and a
      'help line' for students who encounter problems during their studies overseas. Students may choose
      a Total Service package, which is available in most HYPS EDUCATION partner offices.
    • Post-arrival services include: free counseling and school placement services for ongoing/further studies,
      travel arrangements for students' families to visit, and various free services provided by
      HYPS EDUCATION branch offices.
    • Free services offered online for further studies, job information, events, financial aid, insurance matters,
      community activities, etc.
